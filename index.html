Ajey Gore

Operating Partner, Technology at PeakXV Partners (FKA Sequoia Capital India & SEA)

My journey spans Group CTO at Gojek (GoTo Group), Head of Technology at ThoughtWorks, and founding CodeIgnition which was acquired by Gojek. This diverse experience across hypergrowth startups, consulting, and entrepreneurship gives me unique insights into building technology organizations.

I help startups navigate the complexities of scaling engineering teams, establishing robust software architecture, and implementing sustainable engineering practices. Whether it's designing distributed systems, building high-performance teams, or establishing engineering culture, I bring both strategic vision and tactical execution. Most importantly, I remain a hands-on coder who believes great technology leaders never stop writing production-ready code - because the best architectural decisions come from understanding the real challenges developers face.
